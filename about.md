For a living, I create software applications, primarily on the web. With over 15 years of experience (although I've realised experience does not equate expertise), I prefer delivering products following lean and agile (with a lowercase 'a') processes, aiming to reduce waste and focus on value.

I work on a contract-basis in and around Manchester, in the UK. Currently I am enjoying being back working with [Street Support Network](https://streetsupport.net), a small, but influential charity tackling poverty and homelessness in Manchester and beyond.

Outside of software, I enjoy improving my mental and physical well-being through mindfulness, yoga, jiu-jitsu, weights training, running, swimming and cycling, as well as snowboarding, windsurfing and gravel/mountain biking.

After what feels like a lifetime of being hindered by self-esteem and the resultant mental health issues, I am particularly keen on gaining new experiences and broadening my knowledge to make up for lost time!

Currently, my main areas of interest include:

* mental health
* anthropology
* philosophy
* education
