## Sun Tzu's Five Factors when facing an opponent
* Purpose - why should people follow what you do?
* Landscape - description of environment/context
* Climate - forces that affect your environment
* Doctrine - runbooks that can be applied, dependent on climate
* Leadership - choosing the correct runbook

Purpose defines the overall answer to Why? But how does one decide on the path to the Goal?

Context is key!

Process of leadership is iterative, depending on how each of the factors interact and evolve.

## Mapping
### Properties of a map
* visual representation
* context-specific
* position of components relative to
* an anchor point
* depiction of movement

1. Define components in your domain, plotting them on a vertical axis of value, and connecting dependencies
2. Position each component on a horizontal axis of ubiquity: bleeding edge to commodity

![Cheat Sheet](https://miro.medium.com/max/700/1*Hr-7aM2-IW34edpBtnKWQQ.jpeg)

All components eventually evolve from left to right; we are all standing on the shoulders of giants!
